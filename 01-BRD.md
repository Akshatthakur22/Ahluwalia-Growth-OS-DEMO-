# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

**Ahluwalia Growth OS**

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Ahluwalia Growth OS is envisioned as a centralized business operating platform designed to streamline and digitize the core operations of Ahluwalia Marbles. The platform aims to integrate employee attendance, field-force activities, marketing efforts, showroom sales processes, training initiatives, and management reporting into a unified system.

The objective of this initiative is to transform day-to-day employee activities into long-term business assets while improving operational visibility, accountability, relationship management, and revenue generation capabilities.

The system is intended to serve as the operational backbone of the organization and support scalable growth by institutionalizing knowledge and reducing dependency on individual employees.

---

## 2. Business Background

At present, many operational activities are managed using disconnected tools such as WhatsApp, Excel spreadsheets, and personal employee knowledge.

This fragmented approach has resulted in several challenges:

* Critical business information is stored informally.
* Follow-up activities may be missed or delayed.
* Management lacks visibility into ongoing opportunities.
* Employee productivity is difficult to measure objectively.
* Valuable market intelligence is lost when employees leave the organization.
* Revenue opportunities cannot be forecasted accurately.

These limitations hinder the organization's ability to scale efficiently and maintain consistency in execution.

---

## 3. Business Problem Statement

The organization currently lacks a centralized system to manage:

* Employee attendance and field activities.
* Construction site intelligence.
* Architect and builder relationship tracking.
* Showroom lead management.
* Opportunity tracking from discovery to conversion.
* Employee training and knowledge retention.
* Management reporting and business insights.

As a result, information remains siloed, execution becomes inconsistent, and business growth depends heavily on individual efforts rather than structured processes.

---

## 4. Business Objectives

The primary objectives of Ahluwalia Growth OS are:

### 4.1 Centralize Business Information

Create a single source of truth for all operational, sales, and relationship-related information.

### 4.2 Improve Visibility

Provide management with real-time access to employee activities, opportunities, and performance metrics.

### 4.3 Enhance Accountability

Track ownership of opportunities throughout their lifecycle and clearly identify contributions made by different teams.

### 4.4 Strengthen Relationship Management

Enable systematic engagement with architects, builders, owners, and showroom visitors.

### 4.5 Preserve Organizational Knowledge

Ensure that market intelligence collected by employees becomes a permanent business asset.

### 4.6 Increase Revenue Opportunities

Improve conversion rates through structured follow-ups, better opportunity tracking, and enhanced coordination among teams.

### 4.7 Support Future Growth

Establish a technology foundation capable of supporting advanced analytics and AI-driven capabilities in future phases.

---

## 5. Strategic Vision

The long-term vision of Growth OS is to become the primary operating platform for Ahluwalia Marbles by unifying:

* Marketing activities,
* Field operations,
* Showroom sales processes,
* Employee development initiatives,
* Management oversight,
* Market intelligence gathering.

The platform should evolve into a comprehensive business infrastructure that enables data-driven decision-making and sustainable growth.

---

## 6. Scope of Business Functions

The following functional areas are identified within the scope of the initiative.

### 6.1 Attendance and Live Tracking

The system shall support:

* Employee login,
* GPS-based attendance marking,
* Location validation,
* Route tracking,
* Productivity reporting.

---

### 6.2 Field Intelligence Management

The system shall support collection of:

* Site information,
* Owner details,
* Builder information,
* Architect information,
* Project characteristics,
* Competitor intelligence,
* Geo-tagged photographs,
* Field observations and remarks.

---

### 6.3 Marketing Relationship Management

The system shall support:

* Recording of meetings,
* Relationship tracking,
* Opportunity evaluation,
* Follow-up scheduling,
* Monitoring of engagement efforts.

---

### 6.4 Showroom Customer Management

The system shall support:

* Registration of showroom visitors,
* Requirement analysis,
* Material selection tracking,
* Lead qualification,
* Follow-up management,
* Conversion monitoring.

---

### 6.5 Architect Relationship Development

The system shall support:

* Assessment of architect influence,
* Monitoring of loyalty levels,
* Tracking project history,
* Evaluating revenue contributions.

---

### 6.6 Builder Opportunity Management

The system shall support:

* Classification of builders,
* Monitoring project volumes,
* Estimation of future consumption potential,
* Identification of strategic opportunities.

---

### 6.7 Employee Training and Development

The system shall support:

* Delivery of structured learning content,
* Assessment administration,
* Certification tracking,
* Monitoring of employee progress.

---

### 6.8 Management Reporting

The system shall provide visibility into:

* Attendance trends,
* Site visits,
* Meetings conducted,
* Opportunity pipelines,
* Revenue forecasts,
* Conversion performance,
* Team productivity.

## 10. Success Criteria

The project shall be considered successful if it enables the organization to:

* Centralize operational information,
* Improve visibility across teams,
* Establish accountability mechanisms,
* Increase execution discipline,
* Support informed decision-making,
* Create a scalable foundation for future growth.

---

## 11. Conclusion

Ahluwalia Growth OS should be viewed not merely as a software application, but as a strategic business infrastructure initiative.

The platform aims to institutionalize organizational knowledge, improve coordination among teams, strengthen execution capabilities, and provide leadership with the insights necessary to support long-term growth.
