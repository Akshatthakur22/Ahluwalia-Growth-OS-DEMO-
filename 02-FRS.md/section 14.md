# Section 14: Builder Command Center Requirements

---

# Module Name

Builder Command Center

---

# Module Purpose

The Builder Command Center shall enable the organization to assess, prioritize, and strategically engage builders based on their project volumes, consumption potential, and future business opportunities.

The module shall support long-term business planning by identifying high-value builders and improving visibility into their current and projected material requirements.

---

# Functional Requirements

## FR-BLD-001

**Requirement Name:** Builder Categorization Support

**Description:**

The system shall support categorization of builders based on business-related criteria maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-BLD-002

**Requirement Name:** Project Volume Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain project volume information associated with builders.

---

## FR-BLD-003

**Requirement Name:** Business Potential Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain business potential information associated with builders.

---

## FR-BLD-004

**Requirement Name:** Ongoing Project Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain information regarding ongoing projects associated with builders.

---

## FR-BLD-005

**Requirement Name:** Consumption Potential Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain information regarding the material consumption potential associated with builders.

---

## FR-BLD-006

**Requirement Name:** Future Requirement Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain information regarding expected future material requirements associated with builders.

---

## FR-BLD-007

**Requirement Name:** Builder Prioritization Support

**Description:**

The system shall support prioritization of builder engagement activities based on builder-related intelligence maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-BLD-008

**Requirement Name:** Builder Opportunity Visibility

**Description:**

The system shall provide visibility into builder-related opportunity information maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-BLD-009

**Requirement Name:** Builder Intelligence Record Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain records associated with builder growth intelligence and strategic engagement planning.

---

## FR-BLD-010

**Requirement Name:** Builder Performance Visibility

**Description:**

The system shall provide visibility into builder-related business indicators maintained within the platform.

---

# Inputs

The module shall support capture and maintenance of the following information:

* Builder Category Information
* Project Volume Information
* Business Potential Information
* Ongoing Project Information
* Consumption Potential Information
* Future Requirement Information

---

# Outputs

The module shall generate the following outputs:

* Builder categorization information
* Project volume visibility
* Business potential visibility
* Ongoing project information
* Consumption potential visibility
* Future requirement visibility
* Builder prioritization information
* Strategic engagement information

# Business Objectives Supported

The Builder Command Center supports the following objectives:

* Improve builder relationship management.
* Increase visibility into future business opportunities.
* Support strategic allocation of resources.
* Enhance demand forecasting capabilities.
* Prioritize high-value engagement efforts.
* Strengthen long-term revenue planning.

---

# Constraints

The following constraints have been explicitly identified:

* Builder intelligence shall include:

  * Project volume information,
  * Business potential information,
  * Ongoing project information,
  * Consumption potential information,
  * Future requirement information.

* Builder engagement efforts shall utilize builder intelligence information for prioritization purposes.

---

# Open Questions

The following items require stakeholder clarification:

1. What criteria should be used for builder categorization?
2. How should business potential be measured?
3. How should consumption potential be calculated?
4. Over what period should future requirements be estimated?
5. Should builder prioritization be automated?
6. How frequently should builder intelligence be updated?
7. Should inactive builders remain visible within the command center?

---

# Out of Scope (Current Requirement Set)

The following items are not specified within the business requirements document:

* Builder ranking algorithms.
* Predictive demand forecasting models.
* Automated builder segmentation.
* AI-driven prioritization mechanisms.
* External project database integrations.
* Revenue prediction algorithms.
* Resource allocation optimization mechanisms.
