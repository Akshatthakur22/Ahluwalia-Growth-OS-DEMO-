# Section 10: Lead Ownership Module Requirements

---

# Module Name

Lead Ownership Module

---

# Module Purpose

The Lead Ownership Module shall enable the organization to establish accountability throughout the lifecycle of an opportunity by identifying the individuals responsible for creating, nurturing, and converting the lead.

The module shall preserve ownership history, support performance measurement, and maintain revenue attribution information associated with each opportunity.

---

# Functional Requirements

## FR-LOW-001

**Requirement Name:** Opportunity Ownership Recording

**Description:**

The system shall maintain ownership information for every opportunity managed within the platform.

---

## FR-LOW-002

**Requirement Name:** Lead Creator Recording

**Description:**

The system shall record the individual identified as the Lead Creator associated with the opportunity.

---

## FR-LOW-003

**Requirement Name:** Lead Nurturer Recording

**Description:**

The system shall record the individual identified as the Lead Nurturer associated with the opportunity.

---

## FR-LOW-004

**Requirement Name:** Lead Converter Recording

**Description:**

The system shall record the individual identified as the Lead Converter associated with the opportunity.

---

## FR-LOW-005

**Requirement Name:** Revenue Attribution Recording

**Description:**

The system shall record the revenue generated by the opportunity.

---

## FR-LOW-006

**Requirement Name:** Ownership Association Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain the association between ownership roles and their corresponding opportunities.

---

## FR-LOW-007

**Requirement Name:** Revenue Association Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain the association between recorded revenue and the corresponding opportunity.

---

## FR-LOW-008

**Requirement Name:** Ownership History Preservation

**Description:**

The system shall preserve ownership records associated with opportunities.

---

## FR-LOW-009

**Requirement Name:** Opportunity-Level Accountability Support

**Description:**

The system shall support identification of the individuals responsible for various stages of opportunity progression.

---

## FR-LOW-010

**Requirement Name:** Ownership Visibility Support

**Description:**

The system shall provide visibility into ownership information associated with opportunities.

---

# Inputs

The module shall support capture of the following inputs:

* Lead Creator
* Lead Nurturer
* Lead Converter
* Revenue Generated

---

# Outputs

The module shall generate the following outputs:

* Ownership records
* Revenue attribution records
* Opportunity accountability information
* Ownership visibility information
* Performance support information

# Business Objectives Supported

The Lead Ownership Module supports the following objectives:

* Improve accountability.
* Preserve ownership history.
* Increase transparency across departments.
* Support measurement of contribution.
* Enable revenue attribution.
* Strengthen execution discipline.

---

# Constraints

The following constraints have been explicitly identified:

* Every opportunity shall maintain ownership information.
* Ownership information shall include:

  * Lead Creator,
  * Lead Nurturer,
  * Lead Converter.
* Revenue generated shall be associated with opportunities.

---

# Open Questions

The following items require stakeholder clarification:

1. Can the same individual perform multiple ownership roles for a single opportunity?
2. Can ownership assignments change after initial recording?
3. How should revenue be handled if opportunities are partially converted?
4. Should ownership records remain immutable after opportunity closure?
5. Can multiple sales executives contribute to conversion activities?
6. Should ownership information be exposed to all user roles?
7. Should ownership data support future incentive calculations?

---

# Out of Scope (Current Requirement Set)

The following items are not specified within the business requirements document:

* Incentive calculations.
* Commission management.
* Revenue-sharing formulas.
* Automated ownership assignment.
* Ownership dispute resolution workflows.
* Performance scoring algorithms.
* Payroll integrations.
