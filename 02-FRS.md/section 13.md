# Section 13: Architect Growth Engine Requirements

---

# Module Name

Architect Growth Engine

---

# Module Purpose

The Architect Growth Engine shall enable the organization to assess, prioritize, and strengthen relationships with architects based on measurable indicators of influence, loyalty, project potential, and revenue contribution.

The module shall support strategic decision-making regarding architect engagement and relationship development efforts.

---

# Functional Requirements

## FR-ARC-001

**Requirement Name:** Architect Ranking Support

**Description:**

The system shall support ranking of architects based on defined evaluation criteria.

---

## FR-ARC-002

**Requirement Name:** Influence Score Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain influence score information associated with architects.

---

## FR-ARC-003

**Requirement Name:** Loyalty Score Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain loyalty score information associated with architects.

---

## FR-ARC-004

**Requirement Name:** Project Potential Assessment Support

---

## FR-ARC-005

**Requirement Name:** Architect Project History Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain project history information associated with architects.

---

## FR-ARC-006

**Requirement Name:** Architect Preference Tracking

**Description:**

---

## FR-ARC-007

**Requirement Name:** Revenue Contribution Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain revenue contribution information associated with architects.

---

## FR-ARC-008

**Requirement Name:** Relationship Prioritization Support

**Description:**

**Description:**

The system shall maintain records associated with architect growth and relationship intelligence.

# Inputs

The module shall support capture and maintenance of the following information:

* Influence Score
* Loyalty Score
* Project Potential Information
* Project History Information
* Preference Information
* Revenue Contribution Information

---

# Outputs

The module shall generate the following outputs:

* Architect rankings
* Influence information
* Loyalty information
* Project potential visibility
* Project history information
* Preference information
* Revenue contribution visibility
* Relationship prioritization information

# Business Objectives Supported

The Architect Growth Engine supports the following objectives:

* Strengthen architect relationships.
* Improve allocation of relationship-building efforts.
* Increase opportunity identification.
* Enhance visibility into architect contributions.
* Support strategic growth initiatives.

---

# Constraints

The following constraints have been explicitly identified:

* Architect rankings shall consider:

  * Influence information,
  * Loyalty information,
  * Project potential information.

* Architect-related intelligence shall include:

  * Project history,
  * Preferences,
  * Revenue contribution information.

* Relationship-building efforts shall utilize architect intelligence information for prioritization purposes.

---

# Open Questions

The following items require stakeholder clarification:

1. How should architect rankings be calculated?
2. What scale should be used for project potential assessment?
3. What specific architect preferences should be tracked?
4. Over what period should revenue contribution be measured?
5. Should architect rankings be recalculated automatically?
6. Should inactive architects remain visible in rankings?
7. Which user roles should have access to architect intelligence information?

---

# Out of Scope (Current Requirement Set)

The following items are not specified within the business requirements document:

* Ranking algorithms.
* Automated relationship recommendations.
* Predictive architect scoring.
* Incentive mechanisms linked to architect performance.
* External architect data integrations.
* Machine learning-based prioritization.
* Automated architect segmentation.
