# Section 15: Training & Learning Management System (LMS) Requirements

---

# Module Name

Training & Learning Management System (LMS)

---

# Module Purpose

The Training & Learning Management System (LMS) shall enable the organization to deliver structured learning programs, conduct assessments, monitor employee progress, and maintain records of employee development activities.

The module shall support continuous capability building by ensuring employees have access to relevant training resources while providing management with visibility into learning outcomes.

---

# Functional Requirements

## FR-LMS-001

**Requirement Name:** Training Program Availability

**Description:**

The system shall support delivery of training programs to employees.

---

## FR-LMS-002

**Requirement Name:** Employee Training Access

**Description:**

The system shall allow employees to access training materials made available through the platform.

---

## FR-LMS-003

**Requirement Name:** Assessment Administration

**Description:**

The system shall support administration of assessments associated with training activities.

---

## FR-LMS-004

**Requirement Name:** Assessment Participation Support

**Description:**

The system shall allow employees to participate in assessments associated with training programs.

---

## FR-LMS-005

**Requirement Name:** Employee Progress Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain records of employee learning progress associated with training activities.

---

## FR-LMS-006

**Requirement Name:** Certification Tracking

**Description:**

The system shall maintain records of certifications associated with employee learning activities.

---

## FR-LMS-007

**Requirement Name:** Employee Development Visibility

**Description:**

The system shall provide visibility into employee development information maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-LMS-008

**Requirement Name:** Learning Record Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain records associated with employee learning activities.

---

## FR-LMS-009

**Requirement Name:** Assessment Record Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain records associated with employee assessments.

---

## FR-LMS-010

**Requirement Name:** Certification Record Maintenance

**Description:**

The system shall maintain records associated with employee certifications.

---

## FR-LMS-011

**Requirement Name:** Management Visibility Support

**Description:**

The system shall provide management personnel with visibility into employee learning activities and development progress.

---

# Inputs

The module shall support the following inputs:

* Training Programs
* Employee Participation Information
* Assessment Information
* Assessment Participation Records
* Employee Progress Information
* Certification Information

---

# Outputs

The module shall generate the following outputs:

* Employee learning records
* Assessment records
* Progress information
* Certification records
* Employee development information
* Management visibility information

---

# Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Role                  |
| ----------- | --------------------- |
| Employees   | Learners              |
| Managers    | Development Oversight |
| CEO         | Executive Visibility  |

---

# Business Objectives Supported

The Training & LMS Module supports the following objectives:

* Improve employee capabilities.
* Promote continuous learning.
* Standardize employee training processes.
* Preserve learning records.
* Increase visibility into workforce development.
* Strengthen organizational competencies.

---

# Constraints

The following constraints have been explicitly identified:

* Employees shall have access to training programs.
* Assessments shall be supported.
* Employee progress information shall be maintained.
* Certification information shall be maintained.
* Management shall have visibility into employee development information.

---

# Open Questions

The following items require stakeholder clarification:

1. What types of training materials should be supported?
2. Can employees retake assessments?
3. What constitutes successful completion of a training program?
4. Are certifications generated internally or uploaded externally?
5. Should certification expiry dates be tracked?
6. Which management roles should have access to employee development information?
7. Should mandatory training programs be enforced?

---

# Out of Scope (Current Requirement Set)

The following items are not specified within the business requirements document:

* Learning content authoring tools.
* Gamification mechanisms.
* Automated learning recommendations.
* External LMS integrations.
* Video conferencing capabilities.
* Certification generation workflows.
* AI-based skill gap analysis.
