# Section 17: AI Roadmap Requirements (Future Scope)

---

# Module Name

AI Roadmap Module (Future Scope)

---

# Module Purpose

The AI Roadmap Module shall establish a future vision for leveraging organizational data generated through Growth OS to support predictive analytics, intelligent recommendations, operational optimization, and enhanced decision-making capabilities.

The module represents strategic enhancement opportunities intended to build upon the operational foundation established by Growth OS.

---

# Functional Requirements

## FR-AI-001

**Requirement Name:** Revenue Forecasting Support

**Description:**

The system shall support future capabilities related to forecasting revenue using information maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-AI-002

**Requirement Name:** Opportunity Prediction Support

**Description:**

The system shall support future capabilities related to predicting opportunity outcomes using information maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-AI-003

**Requirement Name:** Productivity Alert Support

**Description:**

The system shall support future capabilities related to identifying and communicating productivity-related insights derived from information maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-AI-004

**Requirement Name:** Route Optimization Support

**Description:**

The system shall support future capabilities related to optimizing field routes using information maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-AI-005

**Requirement Name:** Decision Support Enhancement

**Description:**

The system shall support future capabilities intended to enhance business decision-making through intelligent analysis of organizational information.

---

## FR-AI-006

**Requirement Name:** Historical Data Utilization Support

**Description:**

The system shall support future capabilities that leverage historical information maintained within the platform.

---

## FR-AI-007

**Requirement Name:** Cross-Functional Intelligence Support

**Description:**

The system shall support future capabilities that utilize information generated across multiple operational modules.

---

## FR-AI-008

**Requirement Name:** Predictive Capability Readiness

**Description:**

The system shall maintain operational information in a manner that supports future predictive enhancement initiatives.

---

# Inputs

Future AI capabilities may utilize information generated from:

* Attendance records
* Route information
* Site records
* Marketing activities
* Showroom activities
* Opportunity lifecycle information
* Revenue information
* Ownership information
* Relationship intelligence information
* Builder intelligence information
* Employee productivity information

---

# Outputs

Potential outputs associated with future AI capabilities include:

* Revenue forecasts
* Opportunity predictions
* Productivity alerts
* Route recommendations
* Decision-support insights
* Predictive intelligence outputs

# Business Objectives Supported

The AI Roadmap Module supports the following objectives:

* Improve forecasting capabilities.
* Enhance decision-making quality.
* Increase operational efficiency.
* Strengthen opportunity prioritization.
* Improve workforce productivity.
* Support data-driven growth initiatives.

---

# Constraints

The following constraints have been explicitly identified:

* AI capabilities represent future enhancements.
* AI initiatives shall leverage information maintained within Growth OS.
* Predictive capabilities shall build upon operational data generated by existing modules.
* AI functionality is not required for initial deployment of Growth OS.

---

# Open Questions

The following items require stakeholder clarification during future planning phases:

1. Which AI capabilities should be prioritized first?
2. What level of forecasting accuracy is expected?
3. How should productivity alerts be communicated?
4. What constraints should govern route optimization recommendations?
5. Should AI-generated insights require managerial validation?
6. What historical data retention periods should support predictive initiatives?
7. Which user roles should have access to AI-generated outputs?

---

# Out of Scope (Current Requirement Set)

The following items are not part of the initial Growth OS implementation:

* Conversational AI assistants.
* Generative AI content creation.
* Autonomous decision-making mechanisms.
* Automated employee evaluation systems.
* External AI service integrations.
* Real-time recommendation engines.
* Self-learning optimization systems.
