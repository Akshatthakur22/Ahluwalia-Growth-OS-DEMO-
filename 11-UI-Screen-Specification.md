# 1. Purpose

This document defines the user interface specifications for Ahluwalia Growth OS.

The objective is to ensure that the system remains intuitive, efficient, trustworthy, and accessible to employees regardless of their technical proficiency.

---

# 2. UX Vision Statement

Growth OS shall prioritize usability over feature richness, enabling every employee to confidently perform their responsibilities with minimal training.

The platform shall feel:

* Trustworthy,
* Effortless,
* Easy to understand,
* Fast to operate.

---

# 3. Design Philosophy

Growth OS shall adopt a design language inspired by:

* Apple → Simplicity & clarity,
* Linear → Speed & consistency,
* HubSpot → CRM usability patterns.

---

# 4. Design Principles

## DP-001

Simplicity over sophistication.

---

## DP-002

Show only what users need.

---

## DP-003

Common actions should require no more than three interactions.

---

## DP-004

Information shall be progressively disclosed.

---

## DP-005

Mobile-first design shall guide field workflows.

---

## DP-006

Consistency shall be maintained across all modules.

---

# 15. Dashboard Standards

Dashboards shall prioritize:

* Actionability.
* Clarity.
* Simplicity.

Dashboards shall avoid:

* Excessive charts.
* Information overload.

---

# 16. Form Standards

Forms shall support:

* Step-by-step completion.
* Validation feedback.
* Draft saving.
* Camera integration where applicable.

---

# 17. Status Indicators

Statuses shall be displayed using consistent chips.

Supported Lifecycle States:

* New Site
* Relationship Building
* Showroom Visit Scheduled
* Showroom Visit Done
* Selection Done
* Quotation Sent
* Negotiation
* Order Confirmed
* Lost

---

# 18. Accessibility Standards

The interface shall support:

* Clear typography.
* Large touch targets.
* High contrast ratios.
* Minimal cognitive load.

---

# 19. Responsiveness Standards

Mobile:

Primary experience for field users.

---

Tablet:

Supported for showroom operations.

---

Desktop:

Primary experience for Managers and CEOs.

---

# 20. Success Criteria

The UI shall be considered successful if:

* New users require minimal training.
* Common tasks can be completed quickly.
* Information is easy to locate.
* Employees prefer Growth OS over manual methods.
