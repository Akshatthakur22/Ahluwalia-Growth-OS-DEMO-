# 1. Purpose

This document defines the end-to-end business workflows supported by Ahluwalia Growth OS.

The objective is to provide a shared understanding of how work moves across departments and how information flows through the organization.

The workflows described in this document are derived from the approved BRD and FRS documents.

---

# 15. Key Business Principles

1. Every opportunity begins with site discovery.
2. Every opportunity shall have ownership information.
3. Every opportunity shall progress through lifecycle stages.
4. Marketing and Sales responsibilities shall remain distinct.
5. Revenue attribution shall be preserved.
6. Historical information shall remain accessible through search.
7. Dashboards shall provide executive visibility.
