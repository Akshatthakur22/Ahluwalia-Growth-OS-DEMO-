# 1. Purpose

This document defines the dashboard metrics and reporting requirements for Ahluwalia Growth OS.

The objective is to provide actionable visibility into organizational performance, opportunity progression, team productivity, and business growth.

---

# 2. Dashboard Philosophy

Dashboards shall prioritize:

* Actionability,
* Simplicity,
* Clarity,
* Decision support.

Dashboards shall avoid:

* Information overload,
* Decorative analytics,
* Non-actionable metrics.

---

# 3. Dashboard Principles

1. Metrics shall align with business goals.
2. Visibility shall respect role permissions.
3. Metrics shall encourage accountability.
4. Dashboards shall support timely decisions.
5. Historical trends shall be preserved.

---

# 4. Dashboard Categories

Growth OS shall support the following dashboard categories:

* Attendance Dashboard
* Field Operations Dashboard
* Marketing Dashboard
* Sales Dashboard
* Opportunity Dashboard
* Executive Dashboard

---

# 5. Attendance Dashboard

Purpose:

Monitor workforce participation.

Metrics:

* Employees Checked In Today.
* Employees Not Checked In.
* Attendance Percentage.
* Mock GPS Incidents.
* Average Check-In Time.

Audience:

* Managers.
* CEO.

---

# 6. Field Operations Dashboard

Purpose:

Evaluate field activity effectiveness.

Metrics:

* Sites Captured Today.
* Sites Captured This Month.
* Active Field Executives.
* Average Sites per Executive.
* Competitor Intelligence Captured.

Audience:

* Managers.
* CEO.

---

* CEO.

Supported Filters:

* Today.
* This Week.
* This Month.
* This Quarter.
* This Year.
* Custom Date Range.

---

# 15. Dashboard Segmentation Filters

Supported Filters:

* Employee.
* Region.
* Department.
* Opportunity Status.
* Lifecycle Stage.

---

# 16. Visualization Standards

The dashboard shall prioritize:

* KPI Cards.
* Trend Indicators.
* Simple Charts.
* Tabular Summaries.

The dashboard shall avoid:

* Excessive visual complexity.
* Decorative graphics.
* Difficult-to-interpret visualizations.

---

# 17. Dashboard Refresh Requirements

Operational Dashboards:

Near Real-Time.

---

Executive Dashboards:

Periodic Refresh.

---

Historical Reports:

Generated On Demand.

---

# 18. Dashboard Permissions

Field Executive:

Personal activity visibility only.

---

Marketing Executive:

Personal activity visibility only.

---

Sales Executive:

Personal activity visibility only.

---

Manager:

Team-level dashboards.

---

CEO:

Organization-wide dashboards.

---

Administrator:

Full dashboard visibility.

---

# 19. Future Dashboard Enhancements

Potential future capabilities include:

* AI-generated insights.
* Predictive revenue forecasting.
* Anomaly detection.
* Goal tracking.
* Performance benchmarking.

These capabilities remain outside MVP scope.

---

# 20. Success Criteria

The dashboard system shall be considered successful if stakeholders can:

* Understand current business performance.
* Identify operational bottlenecks.
* Monitor team productivity.
* Evaluate opportunity health.
* Make informed decisions quickly.
