# 1. Product Overview

Ahluwalia Growth OS is a business operating system designed to digitize and streamline the complete opportunity lifecycle of Ahluwalia Marbles.

The platform aims to connect field operations, marketing activities, showroom engagements, sales processes, employee development, and executive reporting into a single integrated ecosystem.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Currently, business information is fragmented across:

* WhatsApp conversations
* Phone calls
* Excel sheets
* Individual employee knowledge
* Manual reporting processes

As a result:

* Opportunities are difficult to track.
* Ownership becomes unclear.
* Relationship history gets lost.
* Management lacks real-time visibility.
* Revenue forecasting becomes unreliable.

Growth OS aims to eliminate these challenges by establishing a centralized business operating system.

---

# 3. Product Vision

To become the single source of truth for Ahluwalia Marbles by enabling:

* Opportunity discovery,
* Relationship nurturing,
* Sales conversion,
* Performance visibility,
* Strategic business intelligence.

---

# 4. Success Metrics

The MVP shall be considered successful if it enables:

* Digital capture of all newly discovered sites.
* Assignment of opportunities across teams.
* Tracking of opportunities through lifecycle stages.
* Visibility into ownership responsibilities.
* Management dashboards for pipeline monitoring.
* Retrieval of historical information using mobile numbers.

# 8. Out of Scope (MVP)

The following capabilities shall not be included within the MVP release:

* AI-driven recommendations.
* Predictive analytics.
* Automated prioritization engines.
* External integrations.
* Advanced reporting customization.
* Gamification mechanisms.
* Self-learning optimization capabilities.

# 12. Risks

Potential Risks:

* Resistance to operational change.
* Poor data quality during initial adoption.
* Incomplete stakeholder participation.
* Undefined business exceptions.
* Over-customization requests.

---

# 13. Assumptions

The PRD assumes:

* Employees will utilize Growth OS consistently.
* Stakeholders will validate derived workflows.
* Mobile devices will serve as the primary interaction medium.
* The client PDF accurately represents current business operations.

---

# 14. Definition of MVP Success

The MVP shall be considered successful if Ahluwalia Marbles can:

1. Capture opportunities digitally.
2. Track opportunity progression.
3. Monitor ownership accountability.
4. Retrieve historical information efficiently.
5. Gain executive visibility into business performance.
